The Heart & Soul is a boutique fitness studio that offers a variety of group fitness classes, private sessions, boot camps and personal training.
Our studio is located in downtown Portland, Oregon.
We have a community atmosphere and offer group classes such as TRX, Yoga, Bikram, Pilates, and more.
If you like to be challenged and learn new things then please come and see us.
Our friendly and encouraging staff will make you feel at home. We are located at 479 Washington Avenue in the Westside Village (at the corner of Washington and Washington Ave).
